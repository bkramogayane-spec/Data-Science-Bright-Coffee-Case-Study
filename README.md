# ☕ Bright Coffee Shop – Sales Analysis Case Study

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes transactional sales data from **Bright Coffee Shop** to uncover actionable business insights that support executive decision‑making. The analysis focuses on **product performance**, **time‑based sales trends**, and **store‑level performance** to recommend strategies for improving revenue and operational efficiency.

The deliverables were created as part of a **data analytics case study** using SQL, Power BI, and PowerPoint.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
The CEO requested answers to the following business questions:
- Which products generate the most revenue?
- What time of day does the store perform best?
- How do sales trends vary across products and time?
- What actions can improve overall sales performance?
